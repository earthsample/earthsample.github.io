i began creating back in the spring of 2018. this was a turbulent time for me. it was a period where i was recovering from the [-------] of one of my closest friend a year prior.

during this time i was in university, and discovered photoshop from one of the courses i was enrolled in. i found creation brought an outlet for the grief i was experiencing.

as time passed i began to gain passion for life again, and have been developing my practice ever since.

often when i'm out i bring my camera, and take pictures of various objects, natural forms, and textures.

later on i sort through these images and isolate for parts of them i find interesting. from there i piece my findings together. currently, my works consist of layers of photos i take during my daily life.

this past december i finished a grant program under 0x society, the first physical nft gallery in canada. in september i left my home in toronto to live with my grandfather in montreal where the gallery is based.

i'll be living with him for the forseeable future.
