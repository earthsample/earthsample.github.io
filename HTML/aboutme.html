often when i'm out i bring my camera, and take pictures of various objects, natural forms, and textures.

later on i sort through these images and isolate for parts of them i find interesting. my pieces consist of layers of photos i take during my daily life.

recently i finished a grant program under 0x society. i left home in toronto to live with my grandfather in montreal, where the gallery is based.
