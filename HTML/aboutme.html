after suffering the loss of a great friend of mine half a decade ago, i fell into deep grief. this first confrontation with mortality brought me to a place of looking within and exploring the human condition. i came to realize my life had a lack of meaning.

through university i discovered photoshop and started messing around with it. creation brought me a form of therapy and expression i'd never had before. it served as a remedy of sorts to my grief.

...

often when i'm out i bring my camera, and take pictures of various objects, natural forms, and textures.

later on i sort through these images and isolate for parts of them i find interesting. my pieces consist of layers of photos i take during my daily life.

recently i finished a grant program under 0x society, the first physical nft gallery in canada. this past september, i left home in toronto to live with my grandfather in montreal where the gallery is based.

i'll be living with him for the forseeable future.
