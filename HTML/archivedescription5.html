home now | 2019

upon arriving in hong kong, the stark difference of life between my home in ontario and the tropical region were immediately pronounced.

being constantly surrounded by pure urban density was something i quickly came to fall in love with.

during the first week i had arrived there, i created 'home now', a piece created through layering and multiplying images of the towering buildings that define the city.

overjoyed with the change of scene in my life, i plastered 'home now' over the building textures to signify the beginning of a four month experience in my new home.
