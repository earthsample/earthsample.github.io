wilt of grace

my superrare genesis piece.

a reflection on ageing, 'the wilt of grace' was born from observations made during my stay with my grandfather (2021-2022).

at his current age of 87, he is weathered by time, though rich in knowledge and curiosity. he represents an ideal of what i'd like my senior years to look like.

this piece consists of images taken at my grandfather's home and the surrounding area.
