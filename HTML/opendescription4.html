mom's room

in september 2021 i moved to montreal to live with my grandfather. he's had this house since my mother was a child, and hasnt changed a thing about it since the 70's.

i've been living in the same room my mom grew up in. there's a unique feeling of overlapping history that comes from living in this space.

this piece was created solely with photos i took from within my mom's room.
