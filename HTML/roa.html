Random Object Arrangements

Random Object Arrangements is a project I'm currently undergoing. ROA acts a practice of my design skills, allowing me to experiment and engage with objects I otherwise wouldnt. Followers of my instagram send me a handful of random objects, and I create works from them. This project is ongoing - if you'd like to be a part of it, send me an Instagram DM with a couple objects and I'll get to work.
